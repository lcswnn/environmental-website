Charities and Organizations

World Wild Life for Nature

The World Wildlife Fund for Nature (WWF) is a leading international conservation organization working to protect endangered species and their habitats across the globe. Through scientific research, community engagement, and policy advocacy, WWF strives to build a future where humans live in harmony with nature, addressing critical environmental challenges such as climate change, deforestation, and wildlife trafficking.

Greenpeace

Greenpeace is an influential global environmental organization known for its direct action campaigns and advocacy work addressing critical ecological issues worldwide. Founded in 1971, it focuses on protecting biodiversity, preventing climate change, ending nuclear threats, and safeguarding oceans and forests through peaceful protest, scientific research, and policy lobbying, while maintaining financial independence by accepting only donations from individuals rather than corporations or governments.
