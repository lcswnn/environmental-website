Gateway to Green

What is this?

Gateway to Green is a free resource to discover new ways to help keep our planet healthy! Many people want to help the environment, but don't actually know how. This resource was made to let those people know that that even the smallest actions can cause the biggest impacts.

How it works...

Simply look for charities you would like to donate to, volunteering opportunities around you, or environmental media to keep you inspired. You can also learn about how the environment helps and affects us in our everyday life in our Educational section!

Browse our extensive list of charities to donate to! Each organization listed is committed to creating cleaner oceans, preserving lush forests, innovating renewable energy technology, and much more. Your support, regardless of size, contributes to a collective effort to protect our beautiful planet.

Find Charities

Search for volunteering opportunities near you! Taking time out of your day to help keep our planet clean is a great way to help without spending any money. Joining a volunteering group is a great way to make friends and feel like a part of a cause, too!

Browse through restoration projects, wildlife encounters, and beautiful landscapes. The goal of this gallery is to inspire readers and show them the everyday beauty in their lives that they don't typically see, as well as efforts that are taking place to help preserve that information.
